Welcome to OpenNDC

OpenNDC is an open-source community focused in developing and delivering NDC-related software in order to empower this standard.

NDC (New Distribution Capability) is an standard developed and released by IATA to enable airlines to deliver their products directly to third-party consumers.

The only goal of this community is to help developers in the adoption of this new standard.
