Welcome to OpenNDC

We're an open-source community focused in developing and delivering NDC-based software in order to empower this standard.

This is a project dedicated to build open-source software related to the new standard NDC (New Distribution Capability) developed and released by IATA.

The unique goal is to help developers in the adoption of the NDC standard by bringing development tools that helps to meet this goal.
